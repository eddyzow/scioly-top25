About the Poll

Scioly25 is a community-run ranking of Division C Science Olympiad teams curated by former competitors.

A panel of voters submit their top 25 teams each week and the rankings are determined by a points system (1st place gets 25 points, 2nd gets 24 points, ... 25th gets 1 point).
